== Description ==

The Mobile Comply - Word Press Plugin is the first software to give you the power to convert a traditional website to a mobile site 
and then begin a mobile marketing campaign to reach customers with one simple download.  We allow you to begin a mobile marketing 
campaign by converting your traditional Word Press website to a fully functional mobile site in minutes.  We then supply you with 
the ability to start your mobile marketing campaigns by collecting your customer data in your very own mobile marketing database! 
The plugin also provides you valuable statistics on how well your mobile site is doing. What can Mobile Comply do for your site? Mobilize it! 
Mobile marketing, mobile video, mobile geolocation, mobile phone dial are  all within your reach! Download now to get started!

== Installation ==



1. Upload `plugin-name.php` to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
3. Make changes in plugin's options

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

1. Where it my customer data stored?

Answer: On your server.  

2. Will my customer data be used by mobilecomply.com

Answer: No

3. How to I start a mobile marketing campaign from my mobile database?

Answer: You must download another program from Mobile Comply or another service provider to start a mobile campaign with SMS, Push Or Pull technology.
